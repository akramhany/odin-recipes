Shawerma

An arabian meal

Shawerma is a popular Middle Eastern dish that originated in the Ottoman Empire, consisting of meat cut into thin slices, stacked in a cone-like shape, and roasted on a slowly-turning vertical rotisserie or spit.

Traditiionally made with lamb or mutton, it may also be made with chicken, turkey, beef, or veal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Yoghurt
  • Lemon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breast and thigh to 1 and half inch pieces
  2. Add 1 and half garlic clove minced about 1 large
  3. Add 1/8 tsp turmeric
